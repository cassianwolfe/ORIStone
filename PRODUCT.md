# ORIStone

**Your AI co-pilot for Hearthstone.** ORIStone is a desktop overlay that watches your games live and gives you real-time decisions — not just stats. ORI doesn’t just tell you what happened — she tells you what to do.

---

## What it does

### Live Game Overlay
ORIStone reads your Hearthstone game log in real time and instantly reads the board — your minions, your opponent’s, weapons, everything — highlighting what actually matters. When something notable happens, ORI calls it out with a take.

### ORI — Your AI Analyst
ORI is the brain of ORIStone. She's a conversational AI who knows the current meta, knows your deck, and knows the game state. You can ask her anything mid-game — "is this trade worth it?", "what's my win con here?", "what's my opponent probably running?" — and she’ll tell you what she would play — and why.

ORI remembers the conversation. Follow up, push back, ask again. It's a real back-and-forth.

It’s like having a top player sitting next to you — every game.

### Workshop — Deck Builder + Advisor
The Workshop is where you build and refine decks with ORI's help. Browse all Hearthstone cards, build a list, and let ORI analyze it, suggest cuts, or build something from scratch based on what you describe.

Key Workshop features:
- **Full card browser** — filter by class, search, view full card details
- **Deck builder** — drag and build, see your mana curve live
- **ORI chat** — ask about any card, right-click for instant ORI takes, or describe what you want and let her build it
- **Optimize** — one button runs multi-pass optimization on your deck until it stabilizes — with a confidence score at the end
- **Favorites** — save and reload your best decks
- **Chat sessions** — ORI remembers past conversations; switch between them from a dropdown

### Meta Snapshot
ORIStone pulls the current ladder meta — tier list, win rates, play rates, matchup tables — and feeds it into every ORI response automatically so every decision ORI makes is grounded in the current ladder meta. She always knows what’s popular — and what beats it.

### Deck Recognition
Import a deck code and ORI will classify it against known archetypes, show you where it sits in the meta, and give you matchup context.

---

## The experience

ORIStone runs quietly in the background. Open it when you want intel, close it when you don't. The overlay floats over Hearthstone, the Workshop opens as a separate panel. Nothing installs into the game, nothing touches your account.

ORI’s tone is direct and practical — she calls the play. No hedging, no "it depends."
